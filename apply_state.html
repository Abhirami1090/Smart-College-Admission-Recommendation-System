Details of Central Scholarship

R. I. M. C. Dehradun Scholarship, Tamil Nadu
Objective: The R. I. M. C. (Rashtriya Indian Military College) Dehradun Scholarship is awarded by the Tamil Nadu government to support students from Tamil Nadu who get admission into RIMC, Dehradun. This scholarship aims to provide financial assistance to meritorious students who aspire to join the Indian Armed Forces through RIMC.
Eligibility Criteria:
  • The student must be a resident of Tamil Nadu.
  • The student must have secured admission to RIMC, Dehradun.
  • Academic performance and merit-based selection criteria apply.
Benefits:
  • The student must be a resident of Tamil Nadu.
  • The student must have secured admission to RIMC, Dehradun.
  • Academic performance and merit-based selection criteria apply.
How to Apply:
  • Eligible students must submit an application through the Directorate of Collegiate Education, Government of Tamil Nadu.
  • Documents such as admission proof, domicile certificate, and academic records must be provided.
Deceased Government Servants Scholarship, Tamil Nadu
Objective: Support for children of government employees who passed away in service.
Eligibility Criteria:
  • Child of deceased Tamil Nadu government employee
  • Enrolled in a recognized institution
  • Annual family income within government limits
Benefits: Covers tuition, books, and educational expenses.
How to Apply: Apply through Directorate of Collegiate Education.
CENTRAL SECTOR SCHEME OF SCHOLARSHIP (CSSS)
Objective: Financial support for meritorious students from economically weaker sections.
Eligibility Criteria:
  • 80%+ marks in Class 12
  • Annual income < ₹4.5 lakhs
  • Enrolled in UG course
Benefits: ₹10,000 per year for UG; ₹20,000 per year for PG.
How to Apply: Apply online through the National Scholarship Portal.
Scholarship to Son and Daughter of Differently Abled Persons, Tamil Nadu
Objective: Financial assistance for children of differently-abled parents to pursue higher education.
Eligibility Criteria:
  • Resident of Tamil Nadu
  • Parent with 40% or more disability
  • Studying in Class 1–12 or higher education
  • No income limit
Benefits: Covers tuition fees, books, and study materials.
How to Apply: Apply through the District Social Welfare Office with necessary documents.
Scholarship for Differently Abled Students from Class 9th Onwards, Tamil Nadu
Objective: Support for differently-abled students to complete their education.
Eligibility Criteria:
  • Resident of Tamil Nadu
  • Disability percentage of 40% or more
  • Studying in Class 9 or above
  • No income limit
Benefits: Covers tuition fees, stipend for higher education.
How to Apply: Apply at the District Social Welfare Office.
Free Education Schemes for BC, MBC & DNC Students, Tamil Nadu
Objective: Encourages higher education among backward communities.
Eligibility Criteria:
  • Belong to BC, MBC, or DNC category
  • Pursuing Diploma, UG, or Professional courses
  • Annual family income < ₹2.00 lakhs
Benefits: Full tuition fee waiver, hostel & study material assistance.
How to Apply: Apply through respective educational institutions.
Incentive Scheme for Rural MBC/DNC Girl Students, Tamil Nadu
Objective: Encourages rural girl students from MBC/DNC to complete their education.
Eligibility Criteria:
  • Girls from MBC/DNC category
  • Studying in Class 3 to 6 in rural Tamil Nadu
  • Enrolled in a recognized school
Benefits: ₹500 per year (Class 3-5), ₹1000 per year (Class 6).
How to Apply: Through the school headmaster or District Backward Classes and Minority Welfare Office.
Thanthai Periyar Memorial Award, Tamil Nadu
Objective: Award for meritorious BC, MBC, DNC students excelling in Class 12.
Eligibility Criteria:
  • Belonging to BC, MBC, or DNC
  • Highest marks in Class 12
  • Admitted to professional courses in Tamil Nadu
Benefits: ₹10,000 one-time award.
How to Apply: Automatic selection based on Class 12 results.
Perarignar Anna Memorial Award, Tamil Nadu
Objective: Encourages higher education for BC, MBC, DNC students.
Eligibility Criteria:
  • Belonging to BC, MBC, or DNC
  • Highest marks in Class 12
  • Admitted to Engineering/Medicine in Tamil Nadu
Benefits: ₹10,000 one-time award.
How to Apply: Automatic selection based on board exam results.
Vidyadhan Scholarship Program
Objective: Financial support for economically disadvantaged students.
Eligibility Criteria:
  • Completed Class 10 with high marks
  • Family income < ₹2 lakhs
  • Enrolled in Class 11, 12, or UG courses
Benefits: ₹6,000 per year (Class 11 & 12), ₹10,000 - ₹60,000 per year (UG).
How to Apply: Apply online at Vidyadhan Website.
Free Education Schemes for SC/ST Students, Tamil Nadu
Objective: Ensures SC/ST students can pursue higher education without financial burden.
Eligibility Criteria:
  • Belonging to SC or ST category
  • Studying Diploma, UG, PG, or professional courses
  • Annual family income < ₹2.50 lakhs
Benefits: Full tuition fee waiver, hostel and book allowances.
How to Apply: Apply through colleges/universities.
Post-Matric Scholarship for BC/MBC/DNC Students, Tamil Nadu
Objective: Financial aid for students pursuing education after Class 10.
Eligibility Criteria:
  • Belonging to BC, MBC, or DNC
  • Annual family income < ₹2 lakhs
  • Studying in recognized institutions
Benefits: Tuition fee waiver, hostel and maintenance allowance.
How to Apply: Apply online via Tamil Nadu e-Scholarship portal.
Post-Matric Scholarship for SC/ST Students, Tamil Nadu
Objective: Provides financial assistance for SC/ST students in higher education.
Eligibility Criteria:
  • Belonging to SC or ST category
  • Pursuing post-matric education
  • Annual family income < ₹2.50 lakhs
Benefits: Tuition fee reimbursement, monthly maintenance allowance.
How to Apply: Apply via National Scholarship Portal or Tamil Nadu e-District portal.
