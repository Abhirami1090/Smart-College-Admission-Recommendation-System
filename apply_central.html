Details of Central Scholarship

National Talent Scholarship – ICAR
Objective: To encourage talented students to pursue agriculture and allied sciences education.
Eligibility Criteria:
  • Must have cleared AIEEA UG examination conducted by ICAR
  • Should be enrolled in recognized agriculture universities
Benefits: ₹2,000 per month for the full duration of the degree (4 years).
How to Apply: Apply through ICAR official portal after clearing AIEEA.
Swanath Scholarship Scheme
Objective: To provide financial support to orphans, children of COVID-deceased, or martyred armed forces.
Eligibility Criteria:
  • Students should be pursuing technical courses approved by AICTE
  • Family income ≤ ₹8 lakh per annum
Benefits: ₹50,000 per annum for college fees, books, stationeries, and more.
How to Apply: Via National Scholarship Portal (NSP).
AICTE Pragati Scholarship for Girls
Objective: Empowerment of girls through technical education.
Eligibility Criteria:
  • Female student in 1st year of AICTE-approved college
  • Family income ≤ ₹8 lakh/annum
Benefits: ₹50,000/year for tuition, books, etc. for 4 years.
How to Apply: NSP Portal or AICTE website.
Prime Minister's Scholarship Scheme for Wards of States/UTs Police Personnel
Objective: To support higher technical education of police martyrs' children.
Eligibility Criteria:
  • Dependent of martyred State/UT Police personnel
  • Enrolled in professional courses including engineering
Benefits: ₹2,500/month (girls), ₹2,000/month (boys).
How to Apply: Through NSP.
Post-Matric Scholarships for Students with Disabilities
Objective: Promote inclusive education for disabled students.
Eligibility Criteria:
  • Disability ≥ 40%
  • Family income ≤ ₹2.5 lakh
  • Pursuing post-matric technical/professional courses
Benefits: Maintenance allowance up to ₹1,200/month + tuition fees + books.
How to Apply: Through NSP.
Scholarship to BC/MBC/DNC Students, Tamil Nadu (State Scheme)
Objective: Promote education among backward categories.
Eligibility Criteria:
  • Tamil Nadu domicile
  • Must belong to BC/MBC/DNC
  • Parental income ≤ ₹2 lakh
Benefits: Tuition fee waiver and other financial aid.
How to Apply: Apply through Tamil Nadu e-district portal.
Prime Minister's Scholarship Scheme for RPF/RPSF
Objective: Financial aid for children of RPF/RPSF personnel.
Eligibility Criteria:
  • Dependent of retired/martyred RPF personnel
  • Must be enrolled in technical/professional courses
Benefits: ₹2,250/month (girls), ₹2,000/month (boys).
How to Apply: NSP portal.
Merit cum Means Scholarship for Professional and Technical Courses (Minorities)
Objective: Encourage meritorious minority students.
Eligibility Criteria:
  • Belong to minority community (Muslim, Christian, Sikh, etc.)
  • Family income ≤ ₹2.5 lakh
  • Minimum 50% in qualifying exam
Benefits: ₹20,000/year or actual tuition fees. Maintenance ₹1,000/month for hostellers.
How to Apply: NSP Portal.
Post Matric Scholarships Scheme for Minorities
Objective: Help minority students continue post-matric education.
Eligibility Criteria:
  • Minority students from Class 11 to Ph.D.
  • Family income ≤ ₹2 lakh
Benefits: Up to ₹10,000/year tuition fee + maintenance allowance.
How to Apply: NSP Portal.
Scholarships for Top Class Education for Students with Disabilities
Objective: Promote high-quality education among disabled students.
Eligibility Criteria:
  • Disability ≥ 40%
  • Family income ≤ ₹6 lakh
Benefits: Up to ₹2 lakh for tuition + ₹3,000/month maintenance + laptop.
How to Apply: Apply on NSP.
National Fellowship and Scholarship for Higher Education for ST Students
Objective: Enhance participation of ST students in professional education.
Eligibility Criteria:
  • ST students enrolled in technical courses
  • Family income ≤ ₹6 lakh
Benefits: Tuition fee, maintenance, laptop, and more.
How to Apply: NSP Portal.
National Overseas Scholarship for ST
Objective: Financial support to ST students for higher studies abroad.
Eligibility Criteria:
  • ST category, under 35 years
  • Admission in top 500 world universities
  • Family income ≤ ₹6 lakh
Benefits: Tuition, living expense, air travel, contingency.
How to Apply: Apply on https://overseas.tribal.gov.in.
Central Sector Scheme of Scholarship for College and University Students (CSSS)
Objective: Support meritorious students from economically weaker sections.
Eligibility Criteria:
  • 80% marks in 12th standard
  • Family income ≤ ₹4.5 lakh
Benefits: ₹10,000/year (UG), ₹20,000/year (PG).
How to Apply: NSP.
Government of India Scholarship (Generic Term)
Objective: Refers to various scholarships under different ministries.
Eligibility Criteria:
  • Offered by MHRD, Ministry of Minority Affairs, Social Justice, Tribal Affairs
Benefits: Varies by scheme – covers tuition, maintenance, and more.
How to Apply: All schemes are listed and applied through the NSP Portal.
