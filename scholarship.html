About Scholarships

Scholarships for engineering students – The cost of pursuing an engineering course from a recognised college/university generally ranges from ₹8 Lakh to ₹20 Lakh. In government or private colleges, a majority of students find it difficult to meet the costly expenses of the course. This creates a need for financial assistance for students wherein scholarships for engineering students play a crucial role in funding their overall education expenses.

To support aspiring engineers to pursue their dream education, central and state government, numerous universities, private organizations, and corporates are offering a plethora of scholarships and fellowships.
